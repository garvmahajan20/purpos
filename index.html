About Purpos

Purpos was created with one simple vision — to make volunteering easier, smarter, and more meaningful. Across communities, thousands of people are willing to contribute their time, energy, and skills, while countless NGOs and social organizations are searching for dependable volunteers. Yet, many opportunities are missed because there is no simple bridge connecting the two.

Purpos exists to close that gap.

Why We Built Purpos

Many volunteers want to make a difference but often don't know where to begin. Finding trustworthy organizations, discovering opportunities that match personal interests, or identifying causes nearby can be difficult and time-consuming.

At the same time, NGOs often struggle to reach the right people, manage volunteer responses, and maintain long-term engagement.

Purpos was designed to solve both problems through one seamless platform.
